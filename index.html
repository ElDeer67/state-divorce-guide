⚠️ Important: When You Need a Lawyer

These guides are for uncontested, no-fault divorces. You may need an attorney if:

  • Your spouse contests the divorce
  • You have complex property division issues
  • Child custody is disputed
  • There's domestic violence or abuse
  • Significant assets or debts are involved

For simple, uncontested divorces where both parties agree, you can file yourself and save thousands in legal fees.
